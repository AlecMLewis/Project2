Taekwondo

I spent many years from middle school through high school learning Taekwondo. This Korean martial art is regulated by the World Taekwondo Federation from schools to tournaments, all the way to the olympics. They enforce standard practice throughout the martial art.

In August of 2011 I achieved the rank of black belt and had begun training for tournaments. However, a knee injury (unrelated to Taekwondo), caused me to have to quit. Regardless, I learned many lessons throughout my time at the dojang, not just in martial arts but also how to behave as a young man, and have no regrets.

I highly recommend that anyone with even a passing interest find a local dojang and give it a try! Below I have linked a short video that gives a brief overview of the martial art for those who are interested.
